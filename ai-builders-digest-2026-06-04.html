Training Data: Listen Labs — AI That Understands Your Customers
🎧 Training Data
Alfred Wahlforss — Founder & CEO, Listen Labs
June 2, 2026
The Takeaway: As AI makes building things easier, knowing what to build becomes the hard part — and Listen Labs is building the customer intelligence layer for that decision.

Listen Labs is an AI-first customer research platform that can run thousands of voice interviews simultaneously. Founded by Alfred Wahlforss, the company now serves 20% of the Fortune 500 — including Microsoft, Anthropic, Sweetgreen, and NBC — and has conducted over a million AI-facilitated interviews.

The core insight: most product decisions are made without customer input because traditional research is too slow and expensive. Listen's AI agents conduct Zoom-style video interviews at scale, detecting emotional responses through facial expressions and vocal tone — bridging the gap between what people say and what they actually feel.

  • Their AI interview agent achieves 85% on internal quality evals (was 20% with GPT-4) — showing vertical AI's advantage in proprietary evaluation
  • People are more honest talking to AI: "It's a very therapeutic experience because it's a nonjudgmental entity"
  • Building "human API" for agents — coding agents that can call Listen to understand user preferences before building
  • Audience network effects: 30M participants across 200+ countries, profiling expertise across domains from oncology to sneakers
  • Coming next: generative simulation — predict customer responses without running actual interviews, with 95% accuracy for known personas

"Put it another way: as we get closer to AGI, it will be easier to build things, but the hard part will be knowing what to build. And that's what we're building at Listen."

— Alfred Wahlforss, Founder & CEO, Listen Labs
