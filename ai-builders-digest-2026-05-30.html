🔍 Trend Analysis

📈 Three Mega-Trends Shaping AI Builders This Week

1. The Knowledge Graph Revolution — Understand-Anything (+26,685 stars) and CodeGraph (+17,309 stars) together demonstrate the market's hunger for semantic code understanding. AI agents need structured knowledge to be effective, and builders are voting with stars.

2. Agent Ecosystem Maturation — ECC (198K ⭐), Claude Plugins, and the Anthropic Cybersecurity Skills repo show the ecosystem shifting from "can AI code?" to "how do we manage AI agents securely at scale?" Governance (Microsoft's toolkit) and safety (OWASP coverage) are now first-class concerns.

3. Taste & Quality over Speed — Taste-skill (+9K) and stop-slop (+3K) are symptomatic of a maturing market. The community is tired of generic AI output and actively building tools to inject personality, taste, and quality into AI-generated content.
